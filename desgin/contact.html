FAQ

How do I cancel my booking?

You can easily cancel your booking through the website by navigating to the "My Bookings" section and following the cancellation instructions.

What amenities are included in the room?

Our rooms come equipped with essential amenities such as complimentary Wi-Fi, toiletries, and 24/7 room service for a comfortable stay.

Is early check-in available?

Early check-in is subject to availability. Please contact our customer support in advance for any special requests.

How can I modify my reservation?

You can modify your reservation by logging into your account on our website or contacting our customer service team for assistance.

Are pets allowed?

Unfortunately, we do not allow pets in our premises to ensure a comfortable and allergy-free environment for all guests.

What safety measures are in place?

We prioritize your safety with measures such as 24/7 security, regular sanitization, and adherence to local health guidelines to create a secure environment.
